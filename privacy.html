Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Image processing

FormPhotoFit processes uploaded images locally inside your browser. Your image is not uploaded to our server.

2. Personal data

FormPhotoFit does not require login, account creation, or personal information to use the image resizing tool.

3. Feedback forms

If you choose to submit feedback through an external form, the information you provide will be handled by that form provider.

4. Analytics

In the future, FormPhotoFit may use analytics to understand general website usage. Analytics will not access your uploaded images.

5. Third-party links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those websites.

6. Contact

For privacy-related questions, use the contact or feedback link provided on the website.
