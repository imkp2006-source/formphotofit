About FormPhotoFit

FormPhotoFit is a free online tool built to help users resize and compress photos and signatures for online forms.

Many forms ask for images in specific dimensions and file sizes. This can be difficult when your phone camera image is too large or your scanned signature does not match the required format. FormPhotoFit makes this process easier by letting you upload, resize, compress, preview, and download your final image.

Why FormPhotoFit was created

The tool was created to help students, applicants, and everyday users prepare images for exams, admissions, job applications, scholarships, and online document submissions.

What FormPhotoFit can do

  • Resize photos and signatures
  • Compress images under a target KB size
  • Convert output to JPG, PNG, or WebP
  • Preview the final result before downloading
  • Process images locally inside the browser

Privacy-first design

Your image is processed inside your browser. It is not uploaded to any server.
