Photo and Signature Resizer for Forms, Exams, and Applications

FormPhotoFit is a free photo and signature resizer made for online forms, exam applications, admission portals, job applications, scholarship forms, and student documents. Many online forms ask users to upload a photo or signature in a specific pixel size and under a fixed file size such as 20 KB, 50 KB, 100 KB, or 200 KB. This can be confusing when your phone photo is too large or your scanned signature does not match the required dimensions. FormPhotoFit helps you resize and compress images quickly without installing any software.

The tool is useful when you need to resize a photo for an online form, compress a signature under a target KB size, or convert an image into a more suitable format. You can upload a JPG, PNG, WebP, or other supported image file, check its original dimensions and file size, choose a preset, and download the final resized image. If the preset does not match your form requirement, you can enter your own custom width, height, target file size, and output format.

FormPhotoFit works directly inside your browser using image processing with Canvas. Your uploaded photo or signature is not sent to a server. This makes the tool simple, fast, and privacy-friendly. It is helpful for students and applicants who want a quick way to prepare documents without depending on heavy editing software or confusing online tools.

For photos, JPG usually gives the best balance of quality and file size. For signatures, PNG can keep text edges cleaner, while JPG or WebP may help reduce the file size more aggressively. FormPhotoFit also includes a white background option, which is useful when a transparent signature or dark scan needs a clean white background for form upload.

Always check the official instructions of the form you are filling before submitting your final image. Different exams, colleges, and job portals may ask for different dimensions and file size limits. FormPhotoFit gives you a fast way to create a matching image, but the final responsibility is to follow the exact rules mentioned on the official website.

Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about photo resizing, signature compression, file size, and privacy.

What is a photo and signature resizer?

A photo and signature resizer is a tool that changes image dimensions and file size so the image can match online form upload requirements.

Can I compress a photo under 50 KB?

Yes. Upload your photo, set the target size to 50 KB, choose JPG or WebP, and click Resize & Compress. Very detailed images may need lower quality to reach the target.

Can I compress a signature under 20 KB?

Yes. Choose a signature preset such as 140 × 60 px under 20 KB, then process and download the result.

Does FormPhotoFit upload my image?

No. Your image is processed locally inside your browser. It is not uploaded to any server.

Which format should I choose?

JPG is usually best for photos. PNG is good for signatures and sharp text. WebP can give smaller file sizes, but some old form portals may not accept WebP.

Why is my PNG still larger than the target KB?

PNG does not compress with quality settings like JPG or WebP. If your form allows JPG, choose JPG for better compression.

Can I use this for exam forms?

Yes, but always verify the official exam instructions first. Different exams may require different dimensions, formats, and file size limits.

Is FormPhotoFit free?

Yes. FormPhotoFit is a free browser-based image resizing and compression tool.
